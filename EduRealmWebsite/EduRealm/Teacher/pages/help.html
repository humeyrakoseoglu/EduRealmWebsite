    •     Frequently Asked Questions

    • Q1: How can I enroll a course?
      • To register for a lesson, you must enter the page of the course you want to sign up and click on the enroll button
    • Q2: How do I upload course content?
      • If you want to add content to a course you created, click the create course button first. In the window that appears here, decide which of the quiz, document, and video options to add. Once you have decided, click the corresponding button, select your file from your device and upload it.
    • Q3: What is the difference between quiz and exercise?
      • The quiz on our site is time limited, but the exercise is not. You can improve yourself with exercises and test yourself against time with quizzes.
    • Q4: Can we share copies with other people who don't register the EduRealm ?
      • You cannot copy documents (videos, exercise, exam) also you cannot share them. All documents' copyrights belong to academics.
    • Q5: How can I change my password / email?
      • You can click on the settings When the page is open you can see the profile settings section. You can change your new password instead of the old one easily.
    • Q6: How can I change my profile information?
      • To change your profile information, click the settigns section in the navigation menu. Find your profile information in the settings. Write new ones instead of deleting your old information. Finally, leave the settings page by saving the changes.
    • Q7: Can I get a certificate when I finish courses?
      • The courses on our site do not have a final exam. Therefore, you cannot earn certificates from our courses. However, you can benefit from our free education system by watching lessons and doing quizzes at any time.
    • Q8: How can I comment on the course?
      • You can go to the comments page by clicking the comments icon on the page. You can also see the comments of other users and by typing your comment in the text box located here.
    • Q9: Do I need to pay a fee when registering for courses?
      • Edurealm is a platform created to ensure equality of opportunity in education. Our site is not created by profit. Therefore, we do not receive any payments from you.
